Groessenwahn!


AND so goes the story...



This project shows the vision of Moritz and Jakob, two successful architects and friends who decided to quit their job and and follow their dream of running a cafe. They started as a small coffee shop in buchholzstrasse Prenzlauerberg next to Hans Zimmer music school and became one of the local hangout places for musicians to meet and exchange their knowledge. Being jazz enthusiasts themselves Jakob and Moritz eventually saw potential to host live events and support local artists. This led them to buying a bigger place in kreuzberg which we now know as Grossewahn. Grossewahn, over the years, has picked up as one of the livliest cafe-bars in Berlin hosting artists like Dizzy Gilespie and Alan parker.

Grossenwahn also hosts all kinds of art exhibitions and workshops nurturing talent and supporting young artists by providing them a platform to share, collaborate and showcase their ideas. (see events page for more details on upcoming workshops) Größewahn has its own menu and flavor that is hard to beat and is listed as one of Berlins top 10 cafe-bars.
